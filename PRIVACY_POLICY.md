# Privacy Policy for Paint Blok

**Last Updated: July 8, 2026**

## Overview

Paint Blok ("we", "our", or "the app") is a puzzle game developed by Karl Mueller. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect any personal information.**

Paint Blok is designed with your privacy in mind:

- **No account required** - You can play without creating an account
- **No personal data collection** - We do not collect names, emails, or any identifying information
- **No analytics or tracking** - We do not use any analytics services
- **No advertising** - The app contains no ads or ad-related tracking

## Data Stored Locally

The app stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

- Game progress (current level, completed levels)
- Game settings (sound, haptics, dark mode preferences)
- High scores and achievements
- Purchased items and coins

This data:
- Never leaves your device
- Is not transmitted to any servers
- Is not shared with any third parties
- Can be deleted by uninstalling the app

## Children's Privacy

Paint Blok does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

## Third-Party Services

Paint Blok does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
